The Modern Solution to Transplantation

A platform connecting donor hospitals to organ procurement centers through updated digital standards.

Send donor information to an organ procurement organization through our web form.

Form Questionnaire. Provide useful patient information to the OPO.
Scan Documents. Quickly upload required documents digitally.
Send Alert. Securely notify the local OPO of a potential donor.

Meet the App

Access to patient data, donor offers, and consent forms through a single application.

We designed our application to streamline the screening process for donor cases.
